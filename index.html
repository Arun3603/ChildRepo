Welcome to The Child Trust

A philanthropic organization working for over 15 years on the protection and promotion of child rights.

Our Mission

Restoring the lost childhood right—right to life, development, love and care. Rebuilding the trust of children towards the larger society and multiple stakeholders.

We focus on respecting, protecting rights of children, recognition as human beings with human rights, protection against exploitation, trafficking, and the right to free, compulsory, quality and equitable education.

Driving Principles

🏛️

Governance & Child Rights

Focusing on SDGs to attract attention for just allocations and community-based initiatives linking local to global issues.

⚖️

Fair & Just Trade

Promoting ethically based trade practices that empower backward linkages with rural and local economy.

👶

Hidden Children Groups

Addressing street and migrant children—those in need of special protection due to economic policies.

Core Values

  1. A strong belief in children as bearers of Human Rights and recognising their identity
  2. Equity and Justice
  3. Ensuring affirmative actions for marginalised and deprived children, people and communities
  4. Recognise gender equality and encourage girls, women and minorities
  5. Participatory democracy with transparency and accountability
