Motto

“Empowering Rural Minds through Education.”

Vision

To create an educated and empowered rural community where every student has access to quality education and equal opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Mission

To enhance educational opportunities for rural students by providing academic support, career guidance, skill development programs, and access to learning resources, enabling them to pursue higher education and build a better future.

Core Values

Equality

Ensuring equal educational opportunities for all students.

Integrity

Practicing honesty and transparency in all activities.

Empowerment

Enabling students to gain knowledge, confidence, and life skills.

Service

Working with dedication to uplift underprivileged communities.

Excellence

Striving for quality in educational programs and initiatives.

Objectives

  • To support rural students in achieving academic success.
  • To provide guidance for higher education and competitive exams.
  • To conduct spoken English and skill development programs.
  • To organize career guidance and educational awareness programs.
  • To distribute educational materials to economically weaker students.
  • To promote digital literacy and modern learning opportunities in rural areas.
